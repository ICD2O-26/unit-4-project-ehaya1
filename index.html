What is AGI?

Artificial General Intelligence represents the stage where a machine can understand, learn, and apply its intelligence to solve any problem that a human can. Unlike the "narrow AI" we use today (like Siri or ChatGPT), AGI would possess true versatile cognition.

The Vision

The goal is to create systems that don't just predict the next word, but actually understand the "why" behind the world.
