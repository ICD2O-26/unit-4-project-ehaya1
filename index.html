Artificial General Intelligence

Analyzing the shift from task-specific software to autonomous, cross-domain intellectual systems.

The Concept

Current AI models are Narrow. They excel in isolated environments like chess or medical imaging but fail outside them. AGI is the bridge to human-level flexibility—the ability to apply intelligence to any problem without specialized training.

"The question isn't if machines will think, but how we will coexist when they do."
