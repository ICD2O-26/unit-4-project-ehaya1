Artificial General Intelligence

The intersection of silicon and sentience.

The Concept

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) is the hypothetical ability of an intelligent agent to understand or learn any intellectual task that a human being can. It represents a shift from calculating to comprehending.

The Horizon

While current systems excel at specific patterns, AGI aims for cross-domain reasoning and self-aware problem solving.
